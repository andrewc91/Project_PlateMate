About PlateMate

Welcome!


"Don't know what to eat? Oh yeah me too! Let me look it up on Yelp real quick."


Popular apps such as Yelp, Zomato, and others have a strong influence on what people decide to eat. Restaurants with low stars will immediately be overlooked


However, I've been to multiple restaurants that do not have the best reviews, but they have some of my FAVORITE dishes!


I'm not a big fan of keeping "hidden gems" to myself. Therefore this app is to share these dishes for everyone to share!


Not only will this reveal some good meals for food lovers, but I hope it will also bring in more customers for struggling businesses

Andrew Choe

University of Washington - Seattle
